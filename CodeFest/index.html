Learnology

Learnology is about the future. It's about how we can ensure the success of our future. Allow the students of today to become more independant, aware and informed. This includes strenghening their self-learning skills along with helping them work with other peers to ensure a brighter future for all.

LEARN MORE

Within this platform, we provide the students with links of websites and articles that are corresponding with what they are learning at school. This helps them better understand the concepts that they are learning at school, but also promotes self-learning and allows them to challenge given information

SHARE MORE

In this platform, students can share information with each other in the form of articles and links, this would be monitored to ensure the scope of studying. Once, they start sharing they'd learn the scope for international awareness and the need for cooperation with more experienced members having more credibility.

KNOW MORE

This is basically a platform where we provide links to the student so that he/she can widen their general knowledge. This is a way of learning that provides them with greater exposure and the ability to stay up to date. The more they read up, we reward them with points that allow them to have greater credibility when they share information.

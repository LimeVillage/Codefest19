About

This website was created by Nilabh Agrawal and Haresh Wedanayake. It is meant to promote the concept of self-learning as students around the globe seem to depend too much simply on what they learnt at school. The reality is that they need to provide an extra effort and we help them do that. This is a three-pronged approach that involves the need for them to learn what they learn at school in depth. To share their knowledge with their peers around the globe. Or else, even to learn extra knowledge which would provide them with more general awareness. The teachers can also suggest and play a part in what links are being shared. They can evn use some of the sources to help their students further understand. The more they learn, the more credibility they have when it comes to sharing.
